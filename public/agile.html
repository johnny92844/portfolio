Common Activites of A Scrum Team In A Three-Weeks Sprint


Monday Tuesday Wednesday Thursday Friday
Week_#1

Standup Meeting

  • 15 minutes.
  • Update status: What has been done, what will be done, what is blocked.

Sprint-A Tasks

  • Dev: All Dev tasks relate to: designing, coding, review, bugs
  • QA: All QA tasks relate to: test cases, test execution, bugs
  • SDET: All SDET tasks relate to: Test scripts, test execution, bugs

Sprint-A Demo:

  • 2-3 hours.
  • 10 min / each to demo work done.

Sprint-A Retrospective:

  • 1-2 hours.
  • Discuss what went well and what can be improved.

Sprint-B Tasks

  • Team members review to understand Sprint-B user-stories and what need to be done.

Sprint-B Planning:

  • 2-3 hours.
  • Create, plan, estimate, and assign tasks for each user-story backlog.
